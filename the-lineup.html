Methodology: This dashboard represents an independent research practice maintained since early 2025. Data sources include company earnings releases, SEC filings, syndicated industry reports (including Antenna's Premium SVOD 2025 Year in Review), and original modeling. All subscriber economics models use publicly available quarterly disclosures as inputs. Python pipelines (pandas, API integrations) automate data collection from financial APIs with quarterly refresh cadences. Market figures were re-verified against company reports and published Antenna data on July 23, 2026; modeled series (LTV, survival curves, content-spend allocation) carry their vintage in their chart subtitles, and event-log rows added in the July 2026 refresh are labeled as tracked rather than claimed as published analyses.

Coverage: 8 companies tracked with documented assumptions, defined review criteria, and structured analytical output totaling 50,000+ words across thesis documents, viability analyses, and event-driven research.
