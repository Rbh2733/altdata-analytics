# Deep Research Prompt — Funnel Layer (Downstream Intent & Discovery)

> Feed this file directly into Gemini Deep Research or Google Finance Deep Research. It is a prompt, not a report. Do not pre-read `streaming-altdata-ecosystem.md` into the research run beyond the context below, the point is to go past what that map already holds.

## Context (orientation only, do not treat as exhaustive)

This is the smallest and least-detailed layer in both prior research passes: JustWatch, Reelgood, and Sensor Tower track search navigation, watchlist additions, and app-store velocity as leading indicators of subscriber acquisition cost efficiency, particularly around tentpole-title launches when platforms bid up mobile ad inventory to drive downloads. Neither prior pass gave this layer a funding history, ownership structure, or competitive dynamic beyond naming the three vendors, which makes it the layer most in need of an actual deep-research pass rather than a light touch-up.

## Research objective

1. **Ownership and scale, from scratch.** Establish current ownership, funding history, and revenue scale (if disclosed or credibly estimated) for JustWatch, Reelgood, and Sensor Tower independently. These have not been meaningfully researched yet in this project.
2. **Business model clarity.** Do JustWatch and Reelgood monetize primarily through consumer-facing affiliate/referral fees, B2B data licensing to studios, or both? Has either shifted business model since 2024?
3. **Sensor Tower's positioning relative to the Wallet layer.** Sensor Tower is named in both the Funnel layer and as a proxy tool for CAC modeling in the main map's cross-cutting section. Is Sensor Tower better understood as a standalone Funnel-layer vendor or as a general app-analytics company that streaming analysts happen to borrow from? Confirm its primary client base.
4. **Competitive entrants.** Has a new discovery/intent aggregator emerged since 2025 (particularly one built around AI-agent-mediated content discovery, e.g., a user asking an LLM assistant "what should I watch" rather than searching JustWatch directly)? This is the layer most exposed to being disintermediated by conversational AI interfaces; look specifically for evidence of this starting to happen.
5. **Documented predictive accuracy.** Is there any published or credibly reported case where Funnel-layer data (app-store velocity, watchlist adds) was shown to have accurately predicted a subscriber-acquisition outcome ahead of a platform's own disclosure? Prior passes assert this layer is "highly sensitive" as a leading indicator without a single concrete example.

## Execution constraints

- This layer has the thinnest existing base to build on; prioritize primary company sources and named trade press, and be explicit where information genuinely isn't publicly available rather than filling gaps with plausible-sounding inference.
- Every figure needs a source and a date.
- Do not editorialize on investment implications.
