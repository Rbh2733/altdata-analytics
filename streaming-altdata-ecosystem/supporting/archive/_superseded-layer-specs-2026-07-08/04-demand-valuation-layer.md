## 4. Fault lines

**Resolved:**

Luminate's headcount and revenue. Pro states roughly 187 employees. FlashLite states 750-860 employees plus $100 million to $250 million in revenue. Checked against three independent company-intelligence databases (PitchBook, GetLatka, Built In, confirmed via direct WebSearch against PitchBook's own Luminate Data profile and cross-referenced independently), all three converge on 187 employees (LeadIQ separately estimates roughly 193 as of June 2026, consistent within normal estimate variance). Actual reported revenue is around $7.5 million ARR, not $100 million to $250 million. Pro is correct. FlashLite's headcount and revenue figures don't match any database and most likely got pulled from an unrelated entity during generation. Use Pro's 187-employee figure; drop FlashLite's headcount and revenue claims entirely rather than presenting them as a range.

**Open, recorded rather than resolved:**

Luminate's data-point volume: "30 trillion" (Pro) versus "23 to 30 trillion" (FlashLite).

PlumResearch's early backers: Pro names only Tar Heel Capital Pathfinder; FlashLite adds Montis Capital and PFR Ventures.
